Mini Program

The first step in our elite player pathway. Foundations, fun, and a love for the game before the next stage begins.

Our Mini Program is designed as the first step in our elite player pathway, introducing young athletes (U5–U6) to the foundations of the game in a fun, structured, and supportive environment. Sessions focus on building essential skills, confidence, and a love for the sport while gradually preparing players for higher-level training. It serves as a clear progression into our Elite Full-Time Program, which begins at U7, where players take the next step in their development within a more advanced and performance-focused setting.

Built for U5–U6

Age-appropriate sessions tailored to the way young players learn, move, and stay engaged with the ball.

Foundations First

First touch, balance, coordination, and ball mastery introduced through games kids genuinely enjoy.

Fun & Confidence

A supportive environment where kids fall in love with football, build belief in themselves, and look forward to every session.

Pathway to Full-Time

A clear progression into our Elite Full-Time Program at U7, where training shifts into a more advanced, performance-focused setting.
