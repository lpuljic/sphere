Why We Started Sphere Football Academy in Sydney

Built from experience

Sphere Football was created to bridge the gap between Australian football and the standards players face in Europe.

Founder Jeremy Siarakas experienced these differences firsthand when transitioning from the top level in Australia to youth and senior football in Europe.

Having played and coached in Italy and Portugal, established an academy in Cyprus, and completed a two-month training and trial period with Watford, Jeremy gained a deep understanding of what Australian players lack when making the jump overseas.

Through strong connections with Gali Football and player agent Brett Wright, Sphere Football has created genuine pathways to Europe, with proven success in helping players access overseas opportunities.

01

Our Philosophy

At Sphere Football, we believe the game should feel easy on match day because the work has already been done. Every session is intense, detailed, and demanding, so our players step onto the pitch fitter, faster, and smarter than the kids they play against.

02

Our Program

Built on the experience of coaches who have played at a high level in Australia and Europe. They've seen first-hand how European academies train: the standards, the discipline, the obsession with detail. We don't run babysitting sessions or casual kick-arounds. Every drill has a purpose: improving first touch, game intelligence, speed of play, and the physical resilience needed to compete at the top level.

03

Our Goal

To give Australian kids the kind of football education that rivals leading European academies. We want them to dream bigger, train smarter, and play with the confidence that comes from truly elite preparation. If you're looking for a place where your child will be challenged, held to high standards, and coached with genuine professional insight, Sphere Football is for you.

Meet Our Coaches

Built by coaches who have lived the game at the standards young players are chasing.

Technical Director & Founder

Jeremy Siarakas

Sydney FC WSW Youth Europe NSW Futsal

A-League youth systems. Played across Europe. Now building what Australian football development is missing.

Sydney FC WSW Youth Australian Schoolboys NSW Futsal
Italy Cyprus Portugal England

Jeremy Siarakas developed through the A-League youth systems with Sydney FC and Western Sydney Wanderers, alongside selection in Australian Schoolboys, Joeys camps, and multiple appearances representing NSW in futsal. His playing career abroad across Italy, Cyprus, Portugal, and England is where he discovered his passion for coaching, gaining insight into the gaps in Australian player development. Driven by this experience, Jeremy is committed to giving young players the opportunities and guidance he felt were missing, ensuring they don't miss out on reaching their potential.

Head Coach

Johnothan Lynch

NPL Football Futsal 1-on-1

Johnothan Lynch has built his experience through over five years of NPL football across multiple clubs. Runs all 1-on-1 and small group sessions.

Consistently competing at a high level. Alongside his outdoor career, he has excelled in futsal, earning Youth Golden Boot honours and securing two Men’s Championship titles with Hakoah. His success in both formats highlights his technical quality, composure, and attacking impact on the game.
