Futsal Program

Fast-paced, high-intensity futsal across winter and summer seasons with a direct pathway into NPL futsal.

Our futsal program is a 14-week elite offering held across both winter and summer, competing in a high-level academy futsal league. It provides players with additional game time in an intense, fast-paced environment where fewer players on the court mean greater involvement, more touches on the ball, and accelerated technical development. The program also serves as a direct pathway into NPL futsal through our strong connections with Hakoah FC, an NPL 1 futsal club.

14-Week Seasons

Structured winter and summer blocks so players stay sharp year-round, not just during the outdoor season.

High-Intensity Game Time

Fewer players on court means more touches, more decisions, and more involvement every single minute. No hiding.

Academy League Competition

Competitive fixtures in a high-level academy futsal league. Real match pressure that accelerates technical development.

NPL Futsal Pathway

Direct connection to Hakoah FC, an NPL 1 futsal club. Perform here and there's a genuine pathway to the next level.
